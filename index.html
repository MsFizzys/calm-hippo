Massage — Sound Healing — Meditation

Emily Stevens has been practicing intuitive bodywork for over twenty-two years. Her approach is simple — she listens to your body and responds with the techniques it needs. Drawing from a wide range of modalities including hot stone therapy, cupping, gua sha, tuning forks, and sound healing. While working exclusively with organic oils, Emily brings both knowledge and attentiveness to every session.

She works with a diverse range of clients across all ages and circumstances — from those managing serious injuries or physical limitations, to athletes maintaining peak performance, and those simply seeking relaxation and self care. No two sessions are the same, because no two bodies are.

Emily sees clients at her studios in Petaluma and Willits, California. Whatever brings you through the door, you will be met with skill, intention, and respect.
